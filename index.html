Our Story

Fire has always
been the answer

Chef Marcus Hale spent a decade in kitchens built on precision and technique. Then he spent a summer in the mountains of Patagonia, cooking over open fire for a group of strangers, and everything changed.

EMBER was born from that realization: the best flavors come from the simplest methods. Wood. Flame. Patience. We source within 100 miles, change the menu with the seasons, and let the fire do what it does best.
